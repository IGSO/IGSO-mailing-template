Additional info

Using the reference code is important as we will save on transfer fees.

Each participant university must pay their IGSO University participant fee of 10 euros. Please make sure that one of your university team members will bring the 10 euros, which will be collected during check-in. It is also possible for one of the IGSM participants to pay this 10 euro by adding the 10 euros to the payment sum, ie. 120€ + 10€. If you do this, please also drop us an email after making the payment to let us know.

If you are paying for more than one person (as a group), please choose one of you to pay the entire sum using that person's reference number (send normally and change only the sum). After sending the payment, send an email to yourmail@mail.com and tell us who are included in the payment and with whose name the payment was sent.


If we have not received your payment by the deadline, we will first contact you to make sure whether the payment was made before reallocating the place to the next person on the waiting list.

The money that is transferred will not be refundable or transferable to other participants.

We will send you additional information in the coming weeks using email distribution, so please add igsm.fi into the safe recipient list (whitelist) of your email service to make sure you will receive all important information regarding the upcoming IGSM event.
